Overview
“The Water Jug Problem” is a classic puzzle that involves two jugs with different capacities and an unlimited supply of water. The goal of the puzzle is to measure out a specific amount of water using only the two jugs. The capacities of the jugs and the target amount are given as part of the problem's setup.
