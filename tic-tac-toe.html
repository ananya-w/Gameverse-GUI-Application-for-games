Overview
“Tic Tac Toe”, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a timeless two-player game where the goal is to form a straight line of three of your own marks, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, on a 3x3 grid. Players alternate turns, marking an empty cell with their assigned symbol—either an "X" or an "O".
